Introduction

This is a patch (along with content) to allow playback of higher quality, 44.1kHz music in Baldur's Gate II Shadows of Amn with Throne of Bhaal.

Mod Requirements & Details

The current version of the mod supports properly patched installs of Baldur's Gate II Throne of Bhaal. About the content:

The included content is taken from the official soundtrack CD(s) where applicable, arranged to match the original files. As these collections do not cover all music in the game, not all tracks are converted from CD audio quality.

An effort has been made to restore the remaining material, but as with any artificial audio restoration/enhancement your mileage may vary as to how successful the work has been.

In addition to the enhancements, four new tracks have been added; three of which featuring content that is either on the soundtrack but not in the game, or content that was hardly used. The last is taken from the Giants soundtrack by Jeremy Soule. These fill the broken songlist indices 64 through 67, which were coded in the game but had no content. Songlist.2da is assumed to have the vanilla entries untouched.
